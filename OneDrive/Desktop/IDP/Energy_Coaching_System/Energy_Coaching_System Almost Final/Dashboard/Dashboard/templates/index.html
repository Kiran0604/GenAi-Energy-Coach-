Welcome to the Energy Coaching System! This application helps you analyze BLDC motor data, detect issues, and get expert AI-powered recommendations and information about motors and energy schemes. How to Use

  1. Prepare your BLDC motor data as a CSV file with columns like timestamp, temperature, voltage, current, rpm, etc.
  2. Upload your CSV file using the upload form below.
  3. View instant diagnostics, detected issues, and summary statistics for your motor data.
  4. Use the RS775 Motor Datasheet AI Assistant for technical Q&A about the RS775-4538 motor.
  5. Try the RS775 Motor Recommendation Assistant to get actionable advice for detected or custom issues.
  6. Explore the Energy Schemes AI Assistant for information on Indian energy policies, incentives, and best practices.
