Frequently Asked Questions about energy schemes, incentives, and best practices.
Q: What data format is required for uploads?
A: CSV with columns: timestamp, voltage, current, temperature, (optional: torque, energy, rpm).
Q: How do I interpret dashboard alerts?
A: Alerts highlight overheating, voltage drops, current spikes, and efficiency issues. See dashboard for details.
Q: Can I use the dashboard for predictive maintenance?
A: Yes, analytics and ML features can help predict failures and maintenance needs.
Q: Where can I find government schemes for MSMEs?
A: Visit the Guides section for details.
Q: What is the PAT scheme and how does it benefit MSMEs?
A: PAT is a market-based mechanism for energy efficiency. MSMEs can earn certificates and reduce costs. See Guides for more.
Q: How do I ask the chatbot about MSME schemes?
A: Go to the Schemes page and use the AI assistant for personalized answers.
Q: What are the incentives for installing solar panels in my MSME?
A: Subsidies, tax benefits, and reduced energy bills. See Guides and ask the chatbot for specifics.
Q: How do I access tutorials and best practices?
A: Use the navigation bar to visit Tutorials and Best Practices pages for step-by-step help.
Q: Is my data secure on this platform?
A: Yes, your data is stored securely and only accessible to authorized users.
Q: Can I download analytics reports?
A: Yes, you can export your analytics in CSV, Excel, or PDF formats from the dashboard.
Q: What types of motors and equipment can I analyze with the dashboard?
A: The dashboard supports BLDC motors, induction motors, and other industrial equipment with time-series data for voltage, current, temperature, and more.
Q: How do I highlight issue points for voltage, current, and temperature?
A: Upload your CSV and the dashboard will automatically mark issue points on the V, I, T graphs based on threshold analytics.
Q: Can I view individual metric graphs and moving averages?
A: Yes, after uploading data, you can view separate time-series graphs for each metric, including moving averages and insights.
Q: How do I enlarge or fullscreen the graphs?
A: Use the top-right button on each Plotly graph to view it in fullscreen mode for detailed analysis.
Q: What file types are supported for upload?
A: Only CSV files are supported. Ensure your data is properly formatted before uploading.
Q: How do I clear all graphs and analytics?
A: Use the 'Clear Graphs' button on the dashboard to reset all visualizations and start fresh.
Q: How does the AI assistant help with MSME schemes?
A: The AI assistant provides personalized recommendations and answers about government schemes, eligibility, and application processes for MSMEs.
Q: Can I export or share my dashboard analytics?
A: Yes, you can export analytics reports in CSV, Excel, or PDF formats and share them with your team or stakeholders.
Q: Who can access my uploaded data?
A: Only authorized users can access your uploaded data. Data privacy and security are ensured by the platform.
