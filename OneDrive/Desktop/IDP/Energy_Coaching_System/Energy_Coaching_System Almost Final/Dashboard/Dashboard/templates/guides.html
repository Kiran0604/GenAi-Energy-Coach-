Comprehensive guides for MSMEs on energy efficiency, schemes, and compliance.
  • UDAY Scheme (Ujwal DISCOM Assurance Yojana):
    Purpose: Financial and operational turnaround for State Electricity Boards.
    Key Features: Incentives for capacity addition, lower interest costs, mandatory energy audits, smart metering.
    Eligibility: DISCOMs and MSMEs participating in renewable energy projects.
    Learn more
  • PAT Scheme (Perform, Achieve and Trade):
    Purpose: Enhance energy efficiency in energy-intensive industries.
    Key Features: Energy reduction targets, efficiency certificates, trading platform, financial penalties for non-compliance.
    Eligibility: MSMEs in sectors like power, steel, cement, textiles, etc.
    Learn more
  • MSME Energy Efficiency Schemes:
    Purpose: Support for technology upgradation and energy-saving investments.
    Key Features: Capital subsidies (CLCSS), concessional loans, technical assistance, performance-based incentives.
    Eligibility: All registered MSMEs.
    Learn more
  • ECBC (Energy Conservation Building Code):
    Purpose: Set minimum energy performance standards for commercial buildings.
    Key Features: Building envelope requirements, efficient HVAC, lighting standards, solar mandates.
    Eligibility: Commercial buildings and MSMEs with new construction or major renovation.
    Learn more
  • TEQUP Scheme (Technology Upgradation):
    Purpose: Promote technology upgradation and modernization in MSMEs.
    Key Features: Capital subsidy for new equipment, energy-efficient machinery, and process improvements.
    Eligibility: MSMEs investing in technology upgradation and energy efficiency.
    Learn more
  • Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (CLCSS):
    Purpose: Facilitate technology upgradation in MSMEs through capital subsidies.
    Key Features: Subsidy for energy-efficient equipment, process improvements, and modernization.
    Eligibility: MSMEs in specified sectors, including energy efficiency.
    Learn more
  • Rooftop Solar Panel Incentive:
    Purpose: Encourage MSMEs to adopt renewable energy through rooftop solar installations.
    Key Features: Subsidies, tax benefits, net metering, and reduced energy bills.
    Eligibility: MSMEs with suitable rooftop space for solar panels.
    Learn more
