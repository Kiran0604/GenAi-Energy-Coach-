Recommended best practices for energy management and efficiency in MSMEs.
  • Conduct regular energy audits to identify inefficiencies and savings opportunities.
  • Maintain motors, pumps, and equipment to prevent energy loss and downtime.
  • Adopt LED lighting, efficient HVAC systems, and smart controls.
  • Use variable frequency drives (VFDs) for motors to optimize speed and reduce consumption.
  • Monitor power factor and install correction capacitors as needed.
  • Implement waste heat recovery and reuse systems.
  • Train staff and promote a culture of energy awareness and responsibility.
  • Track energy usage with smart meters and analytics dashboards.
  • Integrate renewable energy sources like solar or wind where feasible.
  • Regularly review and update energy policies to align with new technologies and standards.
Quick Tips for MSMEs
  • Schedule equipment operation during off-peak hours to reduce costs.
  • Minimize idle running of machines and switch off when not in use.
  • Fix leaks in compressed air, steam, and water systems promptly.
  • Upgrade to energy-efficient machinery and motors.
  • Insulate pipes, ducts, and tanks to prevent energy loss.
